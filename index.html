ABOUT US

A Timeless Escape

Kelachandra Heritage is more than just a homestay; it’s an experience. Rooted in history and surrounded by lush greenery, our heritage home offers a peaceful getaway where you can reconnect with nature and immerse yourself in authentic Kerala culture. From the traditional architecture to the warm hospitality, every aspect of your stay is crafted to reflect the essence of Kerala. Savor organic, homemade delicacies that capture the flavors of the region. Whether it’s a quiet retreat or a joyous gathering, every moment here is designed to leave lasting memories.
