OUR SERVICES

Discover Serenity and Timeless Elegance

At Kelachandra Heritage, we invite you to step into a world of serenity, tradition, and unparalleled hospitality. Spread across 4 acres of lush greenery, with 2 acres dedicated to creating an unforgettable heritage experience, our venture is a sanctuary for relaxation, celebrations, and rejuvenation.

Weekend Homes

Escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and spend your weekends in tranquility. Our independent villas with private pools are designed to offer you a serene retreat, blending modern comfort with a touch of heritage.

Family Gatherings, Parties & Entertainment

Celebrate life's special moments with your loved ones. Whether it’s a family reunion, a birthday party, or an evening of entertainment, Kelachandra Heritage provides the perfect backdrop for joyous occasions.

Private Functions

Host private functions for individuals or the business community, tailored to your needs. Whether on a weekly or monthly basis, our open spaces and facilities can accommodate your unique requirements.

FACILITIES

Where Tradition Meets Modern Comfort

Discover a harmonious blend of tradition and modern convenience with our thoughtfully designed facilities. Whether you are here to relax, celebrate, or simply enjoy the serene surroundings, our amenities ensure a seamless and memorable experience for every guest. From luxurious villas to open spaces perfect for gatherings, Kelachandra Heritage caters to all your needs with elegance and efficiency.

Reception & Open Restaurant

Welcome your guests with a grand entry foyer and savor delicious meals in our open restaurant. Enjoy culinary delights inspired by the early 90s, prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Independent Villas with Private Pools

Our 6 luxurious villas offer a perfect blend of heritage charm and modern amenities, each featuring a private pool for a truly exclusive experience.

Open Auditorium

An ideal venue for functions, performances, and gatherings, our open auditorium is designed to bring your events to life in a natural, open-air setting.
